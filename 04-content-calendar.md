## Week 6-8: BUILD MOMENTUM

### Strategy: 3 posts/day + 1 TikTok + engage 50 accounts/day

**Content mix (weekly):**
- 5 gym fit checks (different outfits, different locations)
- 4 workout clips/tutorials
- 3 motivation posts
- 3 product features (rotating through the collection)
- 2 community engagement posts (polls, questions, shoutouts)
- 2 lifestyle posts
- 1 transformation/result post
- 1 behind-the-scenes

**Engagement targets (daily):**
- Comment on 30 fitness posts (not "nice pic" — add value)
- Reply to every comment on Nova's posts within 2 hours
- Like 20 posts in target hashtags
- DM 5 accounts for potential collaboration

**Hashtag rotation (use 5-10 per post, rotate weekly):**
- Set A: #fitnessmotivation #gymfit #workout #fitnessjourney #gymlife
- Set B: #fitfam #gymstyle #activewear #fitnessgirl #training
- Set C: #gymwear #fitnessaddict #healthylifestyle #exercise #fitlife
- Set D: #fitnessmodel #gymoutfit #workoutmotivation #gymshark #fitness

## Week 9-12: SCALE

### Week 9: First Paid Ads
- Instagram ad: $5/day — best-performing gym fit post
- Target: women 18-34, fitness interests, US
- Ad creative: "Shop the look" with product tags

### Week 10: Influencer Outreach
- Find 10 micro-influencers (1K-10K followers) in fitness niche
- DM: "Love your content. Sending you a Nova Cruz set — wear it, tag us if you love it."
- Cost: product cost only (~$15 cost per influencer)

### Week 11: Collection 2 Teaser
- "Nova Signature. Coming soon."
- Countdown posts, sneak peeks

### Week 12: Collection 2 Launch + Review
- Launch "Nova Signature" collection
- Month 3 review post: followers, sales, lessons learned
- Set goals for months 4-6
