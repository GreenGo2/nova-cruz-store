# Fit AI Brand — Business Plan

## Executive Summary

**Fit AI Brand** is an AI-powered fitness apparel company. An AI-generated fitness influencer ("Nova Cruz") builds an audience on Instagram and TikTok by posting daily workout and lifestyle content. Her followers buy branded gym clothing from our Printify-powered Shopify store. Zero inventory. Zero model costs. 100% automated.

**Revenue Model:** Direct-to-consumer POD clothing sales + brand deals + affiliate revenue.
**Target:** $3K/month by month 3, $12K/month by month 6, $30K+/month by month 12.

---

## The Problem

Building a fitness clothing brand traditionally requires:
- Models ($100-500/shoot)
- Photographers ($200-500/session)
- Inventory ($5K-20K upfront)
- Photo studio / location costs
- Months of content creation before launch

**Total startup cost: $10K-50K minimum.**

---

## The Solution

An AI influencer eliminates ALL of these costs:
- **AI generates unlimited photos** — $0.01/image vs $100-500/shoot
- **No inventory** — Printify POD, print when ordered
- **Content factory** — 3 posts/day, every day, forever
- **One-time creation cost** for the AI persona (~$100 in image generation credits)

**Total startup cost: <$500.**

---

## The Flywheel

```
Daily content posted by Nova Cruz
        ↓

        ↓
Followers buy Nova Cruz branded gym wear
        ↓

```

## Marketing Strategy

### Phase 1: Organic Growth (Months 1-2)
- 3 posts/day on Instagram (gym fits, motivation, workouts)
- 1-2 TikToks/day (workout clips, trends)
- 5-10 Pinterest pins/day (outfit inspiration boards)
- Engage with fitness community (comment, like, follow)
- Goal: 5K followers by month 2

### Phase 2: Paid Acceleration (Months 3-4)
- Instagram/Facebook ads: $10-20/day targeting fitness interests
- Retargeting ads to website visitors
- Influencer collaborations (send free product to micro-influencers)
- Goal: 30K followers by month 4

### Phase 3: Brand Deals (Months 5-6)
- Supplement brands, fitness apps, equipment companies
- Sponsored posts: $500-2,000/post at 50K+ followers
- Affiliate commissions: 5-15% on fitness products
- Goal: 75K followers + $3-5K/month brand deals
